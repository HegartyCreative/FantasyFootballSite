The 18th season of the Fantasy League has just been completed and, after 9 years absence Andrew Leach's latest chargers Elland Back, stroll back into the fray to win the title for the first time. Sir Jez Sliwka's Gateaux finished as runners-up and Sir Mick Dryk's Loughton Knights were the Cup winners. We offer our congratulations to all.

Now let’s take a look at the season’s highlights.

15 Managers attended the Auction, sadly Glen (Chedi Warriors) could not make it but still managed to finish in 10th place. We had 1 manager rejoin the fold as Andrew Leach (Elland Back) reclaimed his Fantasy League place. The Auction meeting was held for the 10th year running in the upstairs private room at The Calthorpe Arms, Grays Inn Road.

The top buys at the Auction were: £26 million S. Aguero (Man City) Jose Garcia, £21 million Sado Mane (Liverpool) Andy Cummins, £20 million D. Costa (Chelsea) Ian Henry, £20 million Lukaku (Everton) Damon Budds and £18 million Ibrahimovic (Man Utd) by Mark Johnston.

The first week of the season saw, reigning champions, Teletext Town get beat 3-0 by Loughton Knights. Bray Head drew 1-1 with Verry Tenables who scored the 1st goal of the Fantasy Season through Lukakku on the Friday night. Elland Back beat Real Deportivo 1-0, Puma Kings won 2-1 against Dynamo Redhill and Silent Bidders drew 2-2 with Kick up the R's.

The second week saw Elland Back thrash Puskas 5-0, Loughton Knights beat Puma Kings 5-2 and Gateaux won 1-0 against Silent Bidders. Top of the league were Loughton Knights with 6pts, Elland Back 6pts, and Gateaux with 6pts. At the bottom of the league were Puskas, Chedi Warriors and Real Deportivo all on zero points.

Week 4 saw Real Deportivo beat Hophead 3-1, Kick up the R's beat Loughton Knights 2-0, Puskas drew 2-2 with Klopptomaniacs and Chedi Warriors drew 0-0 with Elland Back.

Week 6 saw Bray Head win 3-1 against Teletext Town and Shades of Grays win 1-0 over Real Deportivo. Klopptomaniacs was the only team yet to win. The league standings were: (1) Elland Back 13pts, (2) Gateaux 13pts, (3) Kick up the R's 11pts and (4) Shades of Grays 11 pts.

Week 8 results saw Kick up the R's beat Gateaux 3-0, Hophead beat Puskas 1-0, Loughton Knights beat Klopptomaniacs 4-0 and Elland Back beat Puma Kings 2-0. The table now looked like this (1) Elland Back 20pts, (2) Kick up the R's 17pts, (3) Gateaux 16pts and (4) Shades of Grays 15 pts. Klopptomaniacs were still at the foot of the table without a win with 4pts.

Weeks 9 and 10 were the first 2 games in the group Cup games. Chedi Warriors beat Hophead 3-1, Dynamo Redhill beat Real Deportivo 3-0, Shades of Grays won 2-0 against Verry Tenables, Kick up the R's won 2-0 against Elland Back, Gateaux beat Dynamo Redhill 2-1, Silent Bidders won 2-1 against Verry Tenables and Klopptomaniacs won 1-0 against Kick up the R's.

Week 11 saw Elland Back get beat 1-0 by Bray Head. Verry Tenables hammered Dynamo Redhill 5-1, Klopptomaniacs got his first league win 2-0 over Real Deportivo. Silent Bidders won 3-1 against Teletext Town and Puma Kings beat Puskas 2-1. Top of the table looked like this: Elland Back 20pts, Shades of Grays 18pts, Kick up the R’s 17pts and Gateaux 16pts.

Week 13 was the final Cup group matches. Hophead won 2-1 against Puskas, Bray Head won 3-1 against Chedi Warriors, Real Deportivo won 2-1 against Gateaux, Loughton Knights won 4-2 against Dynamo Redhill, and Kick up the R's beatTeletext Town 3-0. The group winners were Chedi Warriors, Loughton Knights, Silent Bidders and Kick up the R's, the runners up were Hophead, Gateaux, Puma Kings and Klopptomaniacs. Week 14 saw the Cup quarter finals, and the winners were Loughton Knights 3-1 against Puma Kings. Kick up the R's 5-1 against Hophead. Gateaux 2-0 against Chedi Warriors after a replay and Silent Bidders 4-0 over Klopptomaniacs also after a replay.

Week 15 saw the cup semi-finals take place. Kick up the R's beat Gateaux 3-0 to go through and Loughton Knights beat Silent Bidders 2-1.

Weeks 16 and 17 saw Elland Back draw 3-3 against Gateaux after being 3-1 down. Kick up the R's drew 2-2 with Shades of Grays, Puskas won 2-1 against Teletext Town and Real Deportivo won 3-2 against Silent Bidders. Elland Back beat Kick up the R's 2-1. Gateaux won 2-0 against Puma Kings, Shades of Grays drew 3-3 with Verry Tenables and Hophead beat Bray Head 1-0. The table looked like this (1) Elland Backl 27 pts, (2) Gateaux 23pts, (3) Kick up the R's 21pts, (4) Real Deportivo 20pts.

Weeks 18 and 19 were the first and second legs of The Cup Final the first leg saw Loughton Knights beat Kick up the R's 3-1, and he also won the 2nd leg 4-1 to take the cup on a 7-2 aggregate. This was Mick's second Fantasy Cup win.

Week 20 Klopptomaniacs beat Elland Back 1-0. Gateaux lose 1-0 to Real Deportivo and Kick up the R's win 2-1 against Puma Kings. The following week (week 21) saw the penultimate league games, Elland Back drew 1-1 with Dynamo Redhill and Gateaux won 2-1 against Bray Head . (1) Elland Back 28 pts, (2) Gateaux 26pts, (3) Kick up the R's 25 pts, Teletext Town were bottom of the league with 13pts and in danger of putting up the worst defence of a title defence in Fantasy League history.

The final league games saw Hophead beat Loughton Knights 3-2, Chedi Warriors lost 1-0 to Real Deportivo who finished 3rd, Gateaux beat Shades of Grays 1-0 to finish as runners up and Elland Back drew 2-2 with Verry Tenables to win the league on goal difference. There were also final day wins for Silent Bidders 1-0 over Bray Head, Puskas 3-0 against Dynamo Redhill, Klopptomaniacs 3-0 over Puma Kings and Teletext Town 3-2 against Kick up the R's.


Star team status was achieved by ten teams during the campaign. However the teams that gained the accolade more than once were: Elland Back 5, Kick up the R's 4, Klopptomaniacs 3, Loughton Knights 2 and Chedi Warriors 2.


  • The transfer windows had an average of 8 managers involved
  • This is Andrew Leach's first ever Fantasy League title and also the most points (29) he has achieved in a season. Andrew also jumped up 7 places in the finishing positions table from 14th to now stand 7th.
  • Sir Mick Dryk has joined the band of managers (Mark Johnston & Chris Hannaford) that have won the Fantasy Cup on 2 occasions his last win was back in 2004.
  • Paul Lambertini had his highest ever finish (6th) and his highest points haul of 21.
  • Sir Jez Sliwka moved up 1 place in the all time points list to 3rd with 387pts.
  • Jose Garcia also moved in 1 place to 6th in the all time points list with 342pts.
  • Chris Hannaford jumped up 2 places in the all time points list and now sits in 8th place with 335pts.
  • Paul Lambertini moved up 3 places in the all time points list and sits in 20th place with 53pts.
  • Sean Hegarty has officially put up the worst defence of a league title by finishing 16th and last the following season.
  • Finally congratulations to Andrew, Sir Jez and Sir Mick on their achievements this season. Who knows? Next season it could be you!
